O comunitate muzicală pe care te poți baza

SUX Records este o comunitate muzicală din București cu oameni dedicați și cu o experiență muzicală vastă. Muzica este o parte importantă din viața noastră și o tratăm cu respect și seriozitate.

Poza trupei

Not my cup of tea

Not My Cup of Tea is a project shaped between Piatra Neamț and Bucharest, blending alternative rock, shoegaze, and dream-pop into a lo-fi, atmospheric sound.What began in 2019 as a simple jam session slowly evolved into a full band, with members joining from different cities and bringing new layers into the project. Their debut album DWELL, recorded in a small bedroom setting, reflects this process, a raw and intimate exploration of endings, whether personal, social, or emotional. After moving to Bucharest and refining their sound through rehearsals and lineup changes, the band continues to grow, with a second album set for release soon.

Poza artistului

Armand Popa

Armand Popa is a Bucharest-based artist and producer whose sound has evolved from indie-leaning psychedelic rock and shoegaze into a raw, high-energy psychedelic rock direction. Entirely self-produced and shaped by years of writing and producing for other artists, his music is driven by fuzz-drenched guitars and immersive textures. Balancing intensity with atmosphere, his work offers a sharp, critical reflection on modern society and its emotional weight. His upcoming album, recorded at Avanpost Studio, captures this shift and is built for an intense and visceral live experience.
